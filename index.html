When is Stranger Things Season 5 Coming Out?

Netflix has officially announced that Stranger Things Season 5 will be released in three parts:

  • Volume 1: November 26, 2025
  • Volume 2: December 25, 2025
  • Finale: December 31, 2025

Each volume will be available at 5:00 PM PT. Learn more.

Stranger Things Season 5 Cast

The final season features returning favorites and new faces:

  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson
  • Caleb McLaughlin as Lucas Sinclair
  • Noah Schnapp as Will Byers
  • Sadie Sink as Max Mayfield
  • Priah Ferguson as Erica Sinclair
  • Jamie Campbell Bower as Vecna / Henry Creel
  • Linda Hamilton in an undisclosed role
  • Amybeth McNulty as Vickie
  • Nell Fisher, Jake Connelly, and Alex Breaux in new roles

For a complete cast list, visit the Wikipedia page.

Stranger Things Season 5 Episodes
